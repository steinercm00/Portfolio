A little bit about myself...

I was born and raised in New York, and have found my way to Nashville over the years. I am a life-long artist, a shark lover, a sports fanatic and enjoyer of any creative expression.

Having a curiosity in the human condition and emotion lead me to filmmaking and acting, which has taught me invaluable lessons in the connection between human experience and visual communication.

Because of this, I got into design a few years ago and have fallen in love with the processes, concepts and theories of UX/UI between attending bootcamps, reading and practicing concepts in my free time.

When I am not designing, I may be reading, making short films, acting, training at the gym, or hanging out with my dog, Ozzy.
